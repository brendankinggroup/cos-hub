FAQ
Common Questions
How does selling for cash work? +

You fill out our form, we review your property details, and within 24 hours we present a no-obligation cash offer. If you accept, we open escrow with a local title company and close on your timeline — as fast as 7 days. You get paid at closing.

How do you determine your offer price? +

We look at recent comparable sales in your area, the current condition of your home, and current market trends. Our goal is to give you a fair, competitive offer that works for both sides.

Are there any fees or commissions? +

None. We pay all closing costs. There are no agent commissions, no service fees, and no hidden charges. The offer we give you is the amount you receive at closing.

How fast can you close? +

As fast as 7 days if you need to move quickly. Most of our closings happen within 7–14 days, but we can also work around your schedule if you need more time.

Do I need to make any repairs? +

Absolutely not. We buy homes as-is. Roof damage, foundation issues, outdated kitchen — doesn't matter. You don't need to fix, clean, or stage anything.

What if I'm behind on my mortgage? +

We work with homeowners in all financial situations, including those behind on payments or facing foreclosure. We can often close before foreclosure proceedings and help you walk away with cash in hand.
